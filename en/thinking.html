Shift in Thinking

from

Individualistic to Collaborative

Though being a emotional and sensitive person, and working in business enivronment I learnt that the unspoken expectation was that you park your emotional life at the door, and keep things light and professional. In short, you bring a part of yourself to work.

Always questioned myself is this right?

Now in a world of collaboration, it's becoming obvious that people who work together need to have efficient way of knowing and understanding each other, which means sharing more about who they are and how they feel - so that energy that might get wasted in holding ourselves back, hiding our emotional reactions and conflicts can be used in services of furthering the work and creating a more positive work environment.
